Manto

Manto is national food of many countries but uzbekistan manto is so popular.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Chilly
  • Black paper
  • Chaka
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Wheat

Steps

  1. Cut and smash meat
  2. Cut onion and mix it with meat
  3. Add salt
  4. Put meat in wheat
  5. Steam the food
