Kabab

Description

Kabab is an afghan food with delicous taste. its a common food in middle eastern countries and all aroud the world.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • black paper

Steps

  1. Cut and smash meat
  2. Add sauce
  3. Cut garlic and mix it
  4. Add black paper
  5. Grill for enough minutes
